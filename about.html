My Journey

Read My Narrative

I started in operations — not by choice, but by circumstance. At Preplaced, I strategized and launched tiered mentorship offerings, drove a 21% uplift in trial-to-paid conversion, and implemented accountability protocols that reduced unattendance rates by 10%. But somewhere in those process maps and user journeys, I found what I actually loved: understanding how systems fail and fixing them.

At Turing, I lead a high-velocity team of 5-6 analysts executing RLHF and SFT workflows for Fortune 500 AI clients. I've overseen quality assurance for 5,000+ LLM interaction pairs, maintaining >95% data accuracy. It taught me something important — the quality of output is entirely determined by the quality of the process upstream. That's a product lesson, not just an operations one.

Now I'm channeling all of it toward product strategy. Because the best product work is systems work — optimizing "Human-in-the-Loop" data pipelines, driving revenue growth through process automation, and bridging the gap between technical execution and scalable product strategy.
